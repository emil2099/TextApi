Text classification

Alpha

Text classification API allows granular analysis of themes and sentiment.

Our algorithm is different from your typical theme classifier in a way that allows to quickly define themes and concepts using a few words - no tagging and labelling required. This is is important as training data is often difficult to obtain and training labels don't always match the classification task in hand.

In this demo the classifier has been trained to understand the language of banking. It can take customer feedback or complaints and classify them by customer channel, product and root cause

Themes in this demo: online, mobile, branch, telephony, staff, processes, credit card, debit card, lending, overdraft
